# AIOS Wide Robot World

Build: `2026.07.18-world1`

The Guild Hall is now a wide original low-poly game district rather than a narrow presentation strip.

## Character direction

Characters remain the friendly cinematic sci-fi specialist robots from the approved visual concept. They are not block avatars and do not copy another game's characters or assets.

- Rounded capsule body and spherical helmet
- Dark glowing face visor
- Illuminated helmet ring, antenna and backpack
- Router compass, Planner tablet, Builder wrench, Tester lens and Reviewer shield
- Active robot walks between its workshop and the central quest forge

## World direction

- 64 × 48 terrain
- Crossroads and central quest plaza
- Five specialist workshops arranged around the district
- Low-poly trees, path lighting, construction crane and growing quest tower
- Wide overview camera and close follow camera
- Desktop, touch and reduced-power graphics controls

All movement visualizes recorded mission events. The scene does not independently execute AI or terminal operations.
